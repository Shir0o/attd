# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 22, 2026

Thank you for choosing **Attendance Tracker**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Data We Collect

### Attendance Data
The core functionality of the app involves managing member lists and recording attendance for events. This data (including names, event titles, and dates) is **stored locally on your device**. We do not have access to this data unless you choose to sync it with your own Google Drive.

### Usage & Diagnostic Data
To improve app stability and performance, we use **Firebase Crashlytics**. 
- **What is collected:** Stack traces, crash reports, and basic device information (e.g., device model, OS version).
- **Anonymity:** This data does not contain personally identifiable information (PII) like your name or email.
- **Purpose:** To identify and fix bugs and crashes.

## 2. Google Services & Permissions

### Google Sign-In
We use Google Sign-In to authenticate your identity for cloud features. We only access your basic profile information (email and name) to facilitate the connection.

### Google Drive Sync
If you enable the backup/sync feature:
- **Scope:** We use the `drive.appdata` scope. This is a restricted, "hidden" folder in your Google Drive specifically for this application.
- **Access:** The app **cannot** see, read, or modify any other files in your personal Google Drive.
- **Control:** You retain full control over these backup files.

### Google Sheets Integration
If you export reports to Google Sheets, the app will request permission to create and update specific spreadsheets.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
We **do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer** your attendance data to third parties. Diagnostic data is processed by Google (Firebase) solely for the purpose of app maintenance.

## 4. Data Retention & Deletion
- **Local Data:** You can delete all app data by clearing the app's storage in your device settings or by deleting individual records within the app.
- **Cloud Data:** You can delete your sync files by disconnecting your Google account and trashing the application data folder in your Google Drive.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
