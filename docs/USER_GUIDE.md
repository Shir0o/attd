# User Guide

## Introduction
The Attendance Tracker is designed to make event management and member engagement tracking effortless. It prioritizes privacy by keeping all your data in your personal Google account.

## Features

### 1. Hub Dashboard
The Hub is your home screen, providing a bird's-eye view of your organization.
- **Recent Sessions**: View and edit the most recent attendance logs.
- **Quick Actions**: Access member lists, settings, and new session creation.
- **Event Highlights**: See upcoming or recently missed recurring events.

### 2. Member Management
The Members tab is where you manage your roster.
- **Adding Members**: Support for manual entry or bulk family additions.
- **Search & Filter**: Find members instantly as you type.
- **Duplicate Prevention**: The system warns you if a member with a similar name already exists.
- **Soft Delete**: Deleting a member preserves their historical records while removing them from the active list.

### 3. Attendance Taking
The core interaction of the app.
- **Event Selection**: Choose from existing events or create a "custom" session.
- **Swipe Action**: Use the signature "Fluid Humanist" swipe tracks to record attendance status.
- **Real-time Stats**: View the percentage of present members as you go.
- **Labels**: Assign labels (e.g., "Visitor," "Staff") during the attendance flow.

### 4. Google Drive Sync
Keep your data safe across multiple devices.
- **Automatic Sync**: Data is synced on app launch and whenever major changes occur.
- **Manual Backups**: Create timestamped snapshots from the Settings page.
- **Merge Engine**: No data is lost; edits from different devices are intelligently merged using a "last-write-wins" strategy.

### 5. Reporting and Export
Get your data into external tools for deeper analysis.
- **CSV Export**: Generate a standard comma-separated file for Excel or Numbers.
- **Google Sheets Sync**: Direct integration that pushes attendance records to a specified Google Sheet.
- **Analytics**: Built-in charts and graphs showing attendance trends over time.

## Privacy & Security
- **No Third-Party Servers**: Your data is stored on your device and in your private Google Drive folder.
- **Minimum Permissions**: The app only asks for the `drive.appdata` scope, meaning it cannot see any other files in your Drive.
- **GDPR Ready**: Local control over data and easy export/deletion options.
